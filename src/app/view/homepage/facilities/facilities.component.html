Smart Class

Learning with the help of Smart Class technology is fun and easy. Our Online lessons with animations, multimedia modules, video tutorials and practice tests help you comprehend and internalize abstract learning concepts.

Swimming / Splash Pool: is an additional feature of the school. Here the students are taught how to swim and learn the basic concepts of swimming under the observation of trained and dedicated teachers. Separate timings for boys and girls are fixed.
